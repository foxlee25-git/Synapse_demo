Before Synapse

  • Front desk, billing and dentist operate from separate SaaS screens.
  • Patient risk is discovered late, often after chair time is already exposed.
  • Managers ask people for status because the clinic state is not unified.

After Synapse

  • Signals turn into one shared operating state with role-specific views.
  • Every Agent receives only the task, context and approval boundary it needs.
  • Execution, audit trail and outcome update automatically across the clinic.
