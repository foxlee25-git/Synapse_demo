Current role-based shadows

  • Sales Shadow -> CRM, email, pricing docs, follow-up notes
  • Assistant Shadow -> calendar, notes, meeting logs, internal tasks
  • Support Shadow -> FAQ, SOP, support tickets, policy docs
  • Ops Shadow -> weekly reports, campaign sheets, workflow checklists

Configuration Tabs

What each agent needs

  • `Profile` for name, purpose, and role summary.
  • `Data Access` for allowed files, tools, and folders.
  • `Instructions` for how the role should think and write.
  • `Templates` for common outputs like briefs, emails, or summaries.
  • `History` for recent runs, edits, and saved outputs.

Shadow Detail

Sales Shadow example

Configured
Shadow role High-intent lead follow-up Founder-ready weekly brief
Goal

Summarize high-intent leads, draft concise follow-ups, and produce a weekly sales brief.

Data access

Can read CRM exports, last-touch notes, product FAQ, and pricing docs. Cannot read payroll or support inbox data.

Default outputs

Lead summary, ranked list, follow-up email, stalled-deal explanation, and founder weekly brief.

Restrictions

No pricing promises, no direct send, and no changes to the master CRM without review.
